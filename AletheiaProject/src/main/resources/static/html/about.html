About Us

We are Aletheia, an application that was made to help our users to find amazing places to visit and take pictures. Our main objective is provide our users an space to share all that places.

How We Started

We started doing this project with some ideas, the most important of them is that we want we want to provide a space where the user can enjoy the best places in the city, and in turn provide a reality that can be shared with other users of the app, and is also complemented by the value we want to convey that it is to show what is hidden, to show what is true, that place that should be visible to the eyes of the whole world.

What Is Aletheia

Well the idea comes out from the necessity of not having any App that will give all the functionalities all together. On one side we have Instagram that allows us as users to post content to our profile, Google shows us how to arrive at different places and Snapchat shows us a map with different locations. However, none of them have it all in one, so Aletheia does that, it shows you the best locations to take pictures and also how to arrive there and see in the map if the other users are in the same location.

Our Valors and Beliefs

We do this project because we want to provide a space where the user can enjoy the best places in the city, and at the same time provide a reality that can be shared with other users of the app, in addition to being complemented by the value that we want to transmit that it is to show what is hidden, to show what is true, that place that should be visible to the eyes of the whole world.
